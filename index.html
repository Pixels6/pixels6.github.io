Life Hacks for Students

Being a student isn't easy . Trust us, we all know! This site is dedicated to helping Y-O-U succeed, easy-peasy lemon squeezy!

Studying

  1. When studying, use colored pens on keywords to help trigger your memory
  2. Record yourself reading aloud your notes and listen to them
  3. Instead of trying to memorize it you can try explaining to others what you are studying
  4. When studying for a written test, practice writing questions or answers that you think might be on the test to help with remembering
  5. When studying for an exam, Google site.edu (subject) exam to find exams on the same topic.
  6. Don't forget to take breaks , 10 minutes for every 50 minutes spent studying.
  7. Have good time management!
  8. Make study groups with your buddies! Quiz each other and practice together!
  9. Review notes that you took in class
  10. Study with a friend; an opportunity to learn from each other
  11. Practise questions based on the topic to get the hang of it

    12. Super-Duper Study Spaces

  • Switch workspaces when getting distracted
  • On Ambient-Mixer.com, you can listen to the background ambience of fictional worlds, keeping you from getting distracted and studying peacefully.

    4. Plan ahead of time

    Organization

    1. Use an agenda listing important due dates, assignments and homework
    2. Have a checklist or schedule at home with a list of things that must get done
    3. Colour coordinate your notes that can help you remember important points
    4. Highlight important notes

    Motivation

    1. Make attainable goals for yourself and reward yourself when you reach that goal, like more breaks or a treat!
    2. Keep quotes that are inspiring to you
    3. Think about the results and outcome of studying
    4. Think positively! Always find the ray of sunshine in the storm!
    5. Talk it out with someone you trust

    Dealing with stress

    1. Try journaling your thoughts
    2. Take sometime off and do something you enjoy, like maybe a bubble bath or hanging out with friends
    3. Play with a stress reliever like stress balls and squishies
    4. Go on a hike or walk to release stress
    5. Do yoga or something you find relaxing or satisfying
